Plot Summary

What happens when the most trusted secretary of our corporate vice president male lead suddenly hands in her letter of resignation? After working with Vice President Lee Young-Joon for 8 years, Secretary Kim Mi-So announces her resignation from her position as secretary. As Young-Joon tries multiple different ways in order to convince her to stay, he finds himself slowly falling in love with this loveable and hardworking secretary. How would their love story unfold when childhood secrets start to get revealed as well as the rumors and gossip from fellow office workers start to get out of hand.
